Issue
In this article we would learn to use a anchor tag with a webpage and can share it with customers so that they do not have to scan through the page.
While sharing a documentation we can share the modified link to the customer so that it becomes easier for the customer to browse through the page and saves their time.
Environment
All
Cause
Browsing through pages is time consuming and confusing.
We can share the anchored link to the customer so that they can directly visit the desired part of the page.
 
Resolution
Suppose we are sharing a link and ask the customer to visit a the part where it say Define Control Groups:

https://docs.campaign.adobe.com/doc/AC6.1/en/CMP_Marketing_campaigns_Setting_up_marketing_campaigns.html

It would be very confusing and time consuming for the customer to find the part of the page where the customer has to see through the page.

The best way to save time is share the anchored link:

https://docs.campaign.adobe.com/doc/AC6.1/en/CMP_Marketing_campaigns_Setting_up_marketing_campaigns.html#Defining_a_control_group

This way customer can directly jump to the particular part of the page.
